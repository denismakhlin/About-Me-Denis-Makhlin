Denis Makhlin | About Me

I was born on April 26th, 2000 to the parents of Sofa and Anatoliy Makhlin. My first language I learned was Russian, which I am now fluent in. As a child, I was known for having a bright imagination . believing anything and everything was possible. In the beginning of second grade, I began to play basketball on a club team. Finally quitting that team in sixth grade, I joined an AAU basketball team named, “Bay City.” Bay City is a traveling basketball team for people who are skilled in the art of basketball. This team inspired me to understand the hard work and training that has to be put in a sport.

The same year, I joined the Grammy- Award winning, San Francisco Boy’s Chorus. Here I not only learned how to sing and read music, but I also experienced unforgettable moments like singing with the San Francisco Symphony, and performing at schools and opera houses. Singing has been my passion since I was a child, so after I left the Boy’s Chorus, I began to do musical theatre at my school making the lead role in the musical. Music is one of my passions, and I hope to keep it as one of my main hobbies.

This year, I moved from San Francisco to Pasadena. I am now currently a freshman at Pasadena High School. Here I hope to start the future for my career. At PHS, I joined a program named, “App Academy” which teaches computer development and gaming development. Computer science is my main passion, and I hope to pursue a career in the field of it. I hope to become a CEO of a company not only because of the money, but also because it has been my dream ever since I got introduced to the computer.
